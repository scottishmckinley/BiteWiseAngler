Privacy Policy — BiteWise Angler

Effective date: 2026

1. Introduction

BiteWise Angler (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the BiteWise Angler mobile application (“the App”).

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

a) Account Information

When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we collect:

  • Your Google account email address
  • Your display name
  • Your profile photo (if available)
  • A unique user ID provided by Firebase Authentication

We do not collect or store your Google password.

b) User-Generated Content

You may choose to store fishing-related data, including catch records, locations/ports, dates/times, conditions, notes, and photos you upload. This data is stored using Firebase services.

c) Location Information

If you grant permission, the App may access your device’s location to identify nearby ports/locations and provide location-relevant intel. Location access is optional and can be disabled at any time in your device settings.

d) Technical Information

We may collect limited technical data such as device type/OS, app version, and crash/error logs, used only to improve stability and performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate and manage your account
  • Store and sync your fishing data across devices
  • Display personalised fishing intel
  • Improve app functionality and reliability
  • Respond to support requests

We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase, including Firebase Authentication, Firebase Storage, and Firebase databases. We rely on industry-standard security practices provided by Google to protect your information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except for Google Firebase services (for authentication and storage) and where required by law.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting:

bitewiseangler@outlook.com

7. Children’s Privacy

BiteWise Angler is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location (including the UK and EU), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and withdraw consent. Requests can be made via email.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected via updated policy pages.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact: bitewiseangler@outlook.com
