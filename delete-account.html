Account & Data Deletion — BiteWise Angler

BiteWise Angler allows users to request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to bitewiseangler@outlook.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the email address used to sign in to BiteWise Angler

What data is deleted

When your request is processed, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your BiteWise Angler account
  • Authentication identifier
  • Fishing catches and notes
  • Uploaded photos
  • Saved locations and fishing intel

Data retention

Deletion requests are processed within a reasonable time period. No personal data is retained once deletion is complete, except where required by law.
